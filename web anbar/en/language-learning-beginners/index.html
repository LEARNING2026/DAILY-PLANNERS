Language Learning for Beginners: A Step-by-Step Guide

Starting a new language can be daunting. Where do you begin? Grammar? Vocabulary? Speaking? PlanHub simplifies language learning for beginners by breaking it down into small, manageable tasks.

The Beginners Methodology

Our approach focues on the 80/20 rule: rely on the 20% of vocabulary that makes up 80% of daily conversation. We don't overwhelm you with complex grammar rules on day one.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. Memorizing Lists

Don't just memorize random words. Learn phrases in context. Our flashcard system helps you understand usage, not just definitions.

2. Inconsistency

Studying for 5 hours once a week is worse than 15 minutes a day. Use PlanHub's habit tracker to ensure you show up daily.
