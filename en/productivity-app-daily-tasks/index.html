Key Features of PlanHub's Daily Task System

Smart Task Prioritization

Not all tasks are created equal. Our system uses the Eisenhower Matrix principle, helping you distinguish between what is urgent and what is important. Tag your tasks, set deadlines, and add subtasks to break down complex projects into manageable steps.

Integrated Habit Tracker

Success is the sum of small efforts repeated day in and day out. Our built-in habit tracker allows you to monitor your routines, from drinking water to reading 20 pages a day. Visual charts show your consistency, encouraging you to keep the chain unbroken.

Focus Mode for Deep Work

Distractions are the enemy of productivity. PlanHub includes a Focus Mode that minimizes UI clutter and highlights only your current task. Pair this with our Pomodoro timer to work in intense, focused bursts followed by short restorative breaks.
